Privacy Policy

Prompt Maschine  ·  Last updated: April 7, 2026

Prompt Maschine ("the app") is developed by Traian Arion. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Use

The app processes the following types of information:

  • YouTube links — URLs you enter are sent to the YouTube Data API and/or YouTube oEmbed to retrieve public video metadata (title, description). These requests are made on your behalf and are not stored by us.
  • Text you write — Song descriptions, prompts, and other text you enter are sent to OpenRouter's AI API to generate lyrics and Suno prompts. This content is processed by OpenRouter according to their own privacy policy.
  • Generated content — Lyrics and prompts generated by the AI are displayed to you within the app and may be saved locally on your device.
  • App preferences — Settings such as theme, saved profiles, and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports to our own servers
  • Payment information

3. Third-Party Services

The app connects to the following third-party services to provide its core functionality:

  • OpenRouter — AI text generation. See OpenRouter Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube Data API / oEmbed — Video metadata retrieval. See Google Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

4. Data Storage

All data saved by the app (profiles, settings, generated content) is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate any servers that store your personal data.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
